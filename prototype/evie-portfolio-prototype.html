About Me

Hi, I'm Evie.

"My primary goal as a designer is to tell a story through the visuals."

I'm a graphic designer with a passion for visual storytelling. I focus mainly on social media ads and print ads — ensuring that every piece I create conveys exactly the right message.

I thrive in collaborative environments that spark fresh ideas and innovation. I'm always eager to learn and push my creativity further, blending purposeful design with compelling aesthetics.

I'm a recent graduate from the University of Ibadan, where I studied Communication and Language Arts — a background that deeply informs how I think about message, audience, and impact in my design work.

Project Overview

Bloom Cosmetics needed a fresh social media campaign that would resonate with their young, beauty-conscious audience. The brief was to create a series of visually striking ads that communicate luxury without losing accessibility.

The Approach

I developed a visual language centred around soft, feminine tones contrasted with bold typography. Each ad was designed to perform well both on Instagram feed and stories, with a consistent colour palette tying the series together.

Results

The campaign achieved a 42% increase in engagement across platforms, with the hero graphic becoming one of the brand's most-shared assets.
